More

OpenProject offers a variety of features, from document management, to forums and meetings.

 

Integrated documents version control

 
  • Easily access and exchange documents
  • Keep the versions under control
  • Share documents with your team members
  • Lock the documents you are currently working on

Discuss topics with team members in forums

 
  • Create forums to discuss important topics with your teams
  • Add additional forum topics, create discussions and comment on posts

Communicate news to your team

 
  • Communicate the latest news to team members easily and quickly.
  • Read the news from all your projects on the overview page.

Revision and change historys

 
  • All changes are versioned automatically
  • Restore older versions at any time
  • View the changes to documents in the change history

Integration with tickets for easy tracing

 
  • The change history links directly to work packages within OpenProject
  • Trace codes or documents easily to the corresponding project work packages

Create Meetings

 
  • Create new meetings and invite team members
  • Set up recurring meetings
  • Copy existing meetings

Create and maintain meeting agendas

 
  • Create a meeting agenda and send it for to the meeting invitees
  • Link agenda to specific work packages
  • Keep track of the meeting minutes
